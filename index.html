Meet the Artist

Hi, I'm Addy!

A 14-year-old artist, animal lover, and nature enthusiast based in Marin County, California — turning natural henna paste into intricate works of art, one design at a time.

Addy has always had a deep appreciation for the details that others might overlook — the curl of a fern, the markings on a butterfly wing, the geometry hidden in a flower. That same eye for detail is what makes her henna work so special: every line is intentional, every pattern carefully composed.

Her love of animals and the natural world is a constant source of inspiration. Whether she's sketching a tiger, a dalmatian, or a cascading floral vine, Addy brings a warmth and authenticity to her designs that comes straight from the heart. She also expresses her creativity through pottery, where she hand-crafts ceramics and jewelry under her own brand, Adeline & Co.

Every henna design is hand-drawn using 100% natural paste — no stencils, no shortcuts. Just art made with love, patience, and a whole lot of passion.
